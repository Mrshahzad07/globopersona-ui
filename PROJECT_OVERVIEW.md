## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. Interactive Dashboard
- **Real-time Metrics**: Dynamic visualization of campaign performance with count-up animations.
- **Advanced Charts**: Integrated `recharts` for tracking performance trends, volume breakdown, and status distribution.
- **Greeting System**: Personalized user experience with time-based greetings.

### 2. 6-Step Campaign Wizard
A sophisticated, user-friendly flow that simplifies complex operations:
1. **Info**: Campaign basics and goals.
2. **Audience**: Precise industry and role targeting.
3. **Contacts**: CSV import with automated parsing and validation.
4. **AI Generation**: Intelligent email content creation based on campaign data.
5. **Validation**: Deliverability and spam risk assessment.
6. **Launch**: Final review and schedule configuration.

### 3. Contact & Campaign Management
- Filterable tables with status badges.
- Search functionality and bulk actions.
- Detailed view pages for deeper insights.
